Hello!

I’m Nimrod (Nim) Dvir, Ph.D.

I am a Mixed Methods Researcher and Lecturer at the University at Albany’s Massry School of Business, specializing in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and Business Analytics. My work sits at the intersection of data science and behavioral psychology, where I use computational linguistics and AI-driven modeling to understand and optimize how people engage with digital information.
