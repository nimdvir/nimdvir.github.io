Research

Less is More

Explores how the amount of information on marketing landing pages impacts user engagement and decision-making through large-scale online experiments.

More details

Two large-scale experiments (n=535 and n=27,900) conducted in real-world commercial settings. Found negative correlation between information amount and users' decision-making and engagement.
